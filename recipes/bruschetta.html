Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is a traditional Italian appetizer consisting of toasted bread slices topped with a flavorful mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, fresh basil, and olive oil. The bread is typically rubbed with garlic, adding a subtle kick of flavor. This dish showcases the vibrant flavors of ripe tomatoes and herbs, combined with the satisfying crunch of toasted bread. Bruschetta is a delightful and versatile dish, perfect for gatherings or as a light and refreshing snack.

Ingredients

  • Baguette or Italian bread
  • Fresh ripe tomatoes (2-3 medium sized)
  • Garlic Cloves (2-3)
  • Fresh Basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin 0live oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)

  2. Slice the baguette

    Slice the baguette or Italian bread into ½ inch (1.3 cm) thick slices. You can cut them on a slight diagonal for a larger surface area.

  3. Arrange the bread slices on a baking sheet

    Drizzle them with olive oil on both sides.Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake the bread slices for about 10 minutes or until they become golden and crispy. Keep an eye on them to avoid burning.

  4. Dice the tomatoes

    chop the garlic and basil, and combine them in a bowl with olive oil, balsamic vinegar (optional), salt, and pepper.

  5. Add garlic

    Rub a garlic clove over one side of each toasted bread slice

  6. Place tomatoes on bread

    7. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the garlic-rubbed side of each bread slice, spreading it evenly.

  7. Serve and enjoy!

    8. Drizzle with extra olive oil, arrange on a platter, and serve immediately.
