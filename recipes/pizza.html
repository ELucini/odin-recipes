Pizza Margherita

Description

Indulge in the timeless simplicity of Pizza Margherita. This classic Italian pizza showcases a thin and crispy crust topped with vibrant tomato sauce, creamy Fior di Latte from Agerola, and a fragrant touch of fresh basil leaves. The combination of these carefully selected ingredients creates a harmonious blend of flavors, with the sweetness of the tomatoes, the creaminess of the cheese, and the herbal notes of the basil. Whether you're a traditionalist or a pizza lover seeking culinary perfection, Pizza Margherita is a timeless delight that will transport your taste buds to the heart of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fior di latte from Agerola
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible, usually around 500°F (260°C). This ensures a crispy crust.

  2. Shape the dough

    Using your hands or a rolling pin, stretch and shape the dough into a round or rectangular shape, depending on your preference. Aim for a thin crust, but be careful not to make it too thin to prevent tearing.

  3. Add the tomato sauce

    Spread a thin, even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Use the back of a spoon or a ladle to help spread the sauce.

  4. Add Fior di Latte from Agerola

    Tear or slice the Fior di Latte from Agerola, a delicious fresh cheese, into small pieces and distribute them evenly over the tomato sauce. Make sure the cheese covers most of the pizza.

  5. Drizzle with olive oil

    Lightly drizzle some extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza. This adds flavor and helps the ingredients brown nicely.

  6. Bake the pizza

    7. Carefully transfer the baking sheet or pizza stone with the pizza into the preheated oven. Bake for approximately 10-15 minutes or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Serve and enjoy!

    8. Once the pizza is done baking, remove it from the oven, add the fresh basil and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it into desired portions and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious homemade Pizza Margherita!
